1. Overview

Secret PDF is a professional PDF toolkit developed for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS. Unlike most modern apps, we take a radically different approach to privacy: we don't want your data.

This privacy policy explains our commitment to your privacy and clarifies exactly what information we do (and don't) collect, use, or share.

🎯 Our Privacy Philosophy

We believe that your documents are private by default. PDF files often contain sensitive information—contracts, financial records, medical documents, personal notes. That's why Secret PDF was engineered from the ground up to process everything locally on your device, with zero cloud dependencies.

2. Data Collection & Usage

What We Don't Collect

To be absolutely clear, Secret PDF does NOT collect, store, process, or transmit:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • Document Content: We never see, store, or transmit the PDFs you work with
  • Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app, which features you use, or how often
  • Device Information: We don't collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or hardware information
  • Location Data: We don't access or track your location in any way
  • Browsing History: We don't track which files you open or what you do with them
  • Crash Reports: We don't collect crash data or diagnostic information
  • Marketing Data: We don't build user profiles or track you across apps/websites

What We Might Collect (Optionally)

The only data we may receive is:

  • App Store Purchase Information: If you purchase the app or in-app features, Apple processes the transaction. We only receive anonymous confirmation that a purchase occurred, but we don't receive your Apple ID, payment information, or personal details.
  • Support Emails: If you email us for support at xixu@yahoo.com, we'll obviously have access to whatever information you choose to include in your message. We only use this to respond to your inquiry and delete it afterward.

💡 Why This Matters

Most apps send analytics data back to developers—information about crashes, feature usage, user behavior, etc. While this can help improve apps, it also means your data is being collected and transmitted. We've deliberately chosen not to implement any of these systems. Your privacy is worth more to us than that data.

3. Your Documents

Your PDF documents are stored exclusively on your device using the iOS, iPadOS, or macOS file system. Here's what that means:

  • Local Storage Only: All documents remain on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers for this purpose).
  • Your Control: You have complete control over your documents. You can delete them at any time through the app or your device's Files app.
  • iCloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud Drive, your documents may sync to your other Apple devices through Apple's iCloud service. This is entirely controlled by you through iOS/macOS settings, and we have no access to or visibility into your iCloud data.
  • No Cloud Processing: Unlike many PDF apps that upload your documents to cloud servers for processing, Secret PDF performs all operations locally on your device—even advanced features like OCR, conversion, and AI analysis.

✅ Document Security Guarantee

We guarantee that your documents never leave your device through our app. All reading, editing, conversion, scanning, signing, and AI processing happens 100% locally. There are no exceptions to this rule.

4. Security & Encryption

Secret PDF provides multiple layers of security to protect your documents:

Encryption Features

  • Password Protection: You can password-protect any PDF using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. These passwords are stored securely on your device using the iOS/macOS Keychain.
  • Document Encryption: All encrypted PDFs use AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by governments and financial institutions worldwide.
  • App-Level Security: You can enable Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode protection for the entire app, preventing unauthorized access even if someone has your device.
  • Redaction & Mystify: Our redaction feature permanently removes sensitive information from PDFs. The mystify feature allows you to obscure text while keeping documents readable.

Data Protection

  • iOS/macOS Security: Secret PDF leverages Apple's built-in data protection, which automatically encrypts files when your device is locked.
  • Secure Memory: Sensitive data like passwords and decrypted content is kept in secure memory and cleared immediately after use.
  • No Backdoors: We don't implement any backdoors or master keys. If you forget a password to an encrypted PDF, we cannot recover it for you.

5. AI Features & Privacy

Secret PDF includes AI-powered features like OCR (Optical Character Recognition), text extraction, summarization, and document analysis. Here's how we protect your privacy with AI:

On-Device AI Processing

  • Local Machine Learning: All AI features use on-device machine learning models powered by Apple's Core ML and Vision frameworks.
  • No Cloud AI: Unlike apps that send your documents to cloud AI services (ChatGPT, Google Cloud, AWS, etc.), Secret PDF processes everything locally.
  • Offline Functionality: All AI features work completely offline. No internet connection is required or used.
  • No Training Data: Your documents are never used to train AI models or improve our systems. The AI models are static and run entirely on your device.

What This Means

When you use features like "Extract Text" or "Summarize Document," the AI analysis happens entirely on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac. The document content never leaves your device, and we have no visibility into what you're analyzing.

🤖 AI Privacy Guarantee

Many "AI PDF tools" upload your documents to cloud services like OpenAI, Google, or Amazon. Secret PDF never does this. Every AI feature is powered by on-device machine learning that runs locally, ensuring your documents remain completely private.

6. Device Permissions

Secret PDF may request the following device permissions. All permissions are optional and only used for specific features:

Camera Access

Why we need it: To scan documents using your device's camera and convert them to PDFs.

How we use it: Camera access is only used when you actively initiate the document scanner. Photos are processed locally and never uploaded anywhere. You can deny camera access and still use all other features of the app.

Photo Library Access

Why we need it: To import images and convert them to PDFs, or to save exported PDFs to your photo library.

How we use it: We only access photos you explicitly select. On iOS 14+, you can grant access to specific photos only.

Files/Documents Access

Why we need it: To open, edit, and save PDF files stored on your device or in iCloud Drive.

How we use it: We only access files you explicitly open through the app. We don't scan or index your file system.

Notifications (Optional)

Why we need it: To notify you when background operations (like large file conversions) are complete.

How we use it: Local notifications only. We don't send push notifications or marketing messages.

Note: You can revoke any permission at any time through your device's Settings app.
